About Us

GS Mutunda is a rural school located in the Mbazi Sector of Huye District. The school has been involved in agricultural training programs, enabling students to grow significant quantities of produce, such as 160kg of crops during a four-month mentorship period. This hands-on approach fosters practical skills and promotes sustainable agricultural practices among students.
