About Us

GS Gasaka is a public school located in the Gasaka Sector of Nyamagabe District. The school has been involved in initiatives aimed at improving the quality of education and fostering collaboration among educators. It has also benefited from projects focused on enhancing sanitation facilities, contributing to a healthier learning environment.
