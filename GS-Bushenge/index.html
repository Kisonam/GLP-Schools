About Us

GS Bushenge is a secondary school situated in the Bushenge Sector of Nyamasheke District. The school has been part of studies focusing on the use of technology in English language learning, indicating its adoption of modern teaching methods. Its proximity to Bushenge Provincial Hospital allows for collaborative health education initiatives.
