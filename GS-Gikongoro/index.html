About Us

GS Gikongoro is a government-aided school located in the Gasaka Sector of Nyamagabe District. The school offers pre-primary, primary, and secondary education, serving a diverse student population. It has been part of projects aimed at improving sanitation facilities, such as the construction of ecological latrines, enhancing the health and hygiene standards within the school environment.
