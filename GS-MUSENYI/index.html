About Us

GS Musenyi is a government-aided technical secondary school located in the Musenyi Sector of Bugesera District. The school offers programs in agriculture and has been involved in initiatives promoting mental health awareness among students. It serves as an educational hub for the local community, providing both academic and vocational training.
